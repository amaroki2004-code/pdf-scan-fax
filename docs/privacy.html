Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

PDF Scanner ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

Documents you scan are stored locally on your device only. We do not upload, access, or store your scanned documents on our servers.

Purchase information: In-app purchases are processed by Apple. We do not collect or store your payment information.

Usage data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics (e.g., feature usage counts) to improve the app. This data is never linked to your identity.

Data Storage

All scanned documents, PDFs, and signatures are stored locally on your device using iOS app sandbox storage. We have no access to these files.

Cloud Backup (Optional)

If you choose to enable cloud backup, your PDFs are uploaded to your personal Google Drive account. We do not have access to your Google Drive. All uploads are made directly from your device to your own account using your Google credentials.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Apple In-App Purchase – for processing subscription and lifetime purchase payments.
  • Apple Vision Framework – for on-device OCR text recognition. All processing happens locally on your device; no data is sent anywhere.
  • Google Drive API – only used if you choose to enable cloud backup. Your files go directly to your own Google account.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data beyond what Apple processes for purchases, there is no personal data for us to delete or provide. For purchase-related data, contact Apple support.
